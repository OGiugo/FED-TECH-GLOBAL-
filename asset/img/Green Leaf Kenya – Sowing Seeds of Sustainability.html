Green Leaf Kenya is a non-profit organization dedicated to impactful, community-driven change. Unlike others, our focus is on delivering tangible, long-term benefits for the people of Homa Bay County through climate action, environmental conservation, and sustainable agribusiness.

We stand out by putting community needs first, championing inclusivity, and prioritizing solutions that uplift women, youth, and persons with disabilities. Every initiative, resource, and effort goes directly toward building a resilient, greener future—not for profit, but for the community we serve.

Driven by Community, Inclusivity, and Sustainable Impact

At Green Leaf Kenya, our work is shaped by a commitment to the community, inclusive growth, and sustainable progress. We believe in local solutions that empower all members of society—especially women, youth, and persons with disabilities—to lead the way toward a greener, more resilient future. Here’s what sets us apart in our journey to create lasting, positive change.

More Why Choose Us

Sustainable, Not-for-Profit Impact

We focus solely on creating long-lasting change, providing services that support environmental stewardship and community well-being without profit motives.

Data-Driven and Collaborative Solutions

Commitment to Environmental Stewardship

Our Commitment to a Better Tomorrow

At Green Leaf Kenya, our commitment is rooted in a vision of thriving, empowered communities that work together for a sustainable future. We are dedicated to uplifting every community member and ensuring our work reflects our core values: integrity, inclusivity, and environmental stewardship. Guided by these principles, we create lasting impact in every project, program, and partnership.

Through our collaborative approach, we address pressing challenges like climate change, environmental degradation, and economic inequality. By equipping communities with the tools and resources they need to thrive, we build resilience, promote sustainable growth, and inspire collective action for a greener tomorrow.

Frequently Asked Questions.

We’re here to answer your questions! From our mission and programs to ways you can get involved, this FAQ section provides quick answers to help you understand how Green Leaf Kenya is creating sustainable change. Explore below to see how your support and participation make a lasting impact on communities and the environment.

1. What does Green Leaf Kenya do?
Green Leaf Kenya empowers communities through climate action, environmental conservation, and sustainable agribusiness. We provide training, advocacy, and resources to build resilience, promote inclusivity, and create lasting change for communities in Homa Bay County and beyond.
2. How can I support Green Leaf Kenya’s mission?
You can support our mission through donations, in-kind contributions, volunteering, or spreading the word about our work. Every contribution, large or small, helps drive impactful programs that empower communities and protect the environment.
3. Where do my donations go?
Every donation goes directly into our programs, from community climate education and conservation projects to sustainable farming initiatives. We’re committed to transparency, ensuring that every gift has a tangible impact on the communities we serve.
4. How can I get involved as a volunteer?
We welcome passionate volunteers! Whether you’re interested in hands-on fieldwork, community outreach, or providing specialized skills, there are many ways to make a difference. Contact us to learn more about current volunteer opportunities.
Need more info?
Visit our full FAQ page or contact us directly!
